Animancer is an alternative animation system which allows you to control everything in code instead of needing to set up clunky Animator Controllers.

To celebrate the release of Animancer v4.0 I'll be giving away a couple of free vouchers. All you need to do is post a screenshot of one of your Animator Controllers in the Reddit Thread and a quick description of what is working well or any annoyances you have with your setup. Then when that post is about 24 hours old I'll randomly select a couple of people and send them free vouchers for Animancer Pro.
